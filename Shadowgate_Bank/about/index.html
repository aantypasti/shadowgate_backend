Who we are

Shadowgate Bank is the financial arm of FIOC in Prosperous Universe. Our goal is to streamline access to credit, loans, and capital markets for corporations and independent operators, while providing a transparent way to price risk and plan production.

Everything you see here is a player-made system: pages, tools, and data pipelines that connect game-world activity to finance-style workflows.

Explore Tools Home

What we offer

  • Loans — long-term financing for expansion or new freighters.
  • Credit — flexible short-term liquidity with adjustable repayment rate.
  • Futures Exchange (SG1) — trade future delivery of inputs; focus production when contracted.
  • Equity Listings (SG1) — raise funds by listing company shares with a purpose and plan.

Supported currencies: CIS, AIC, NCC, ICA.

How it works

  • Public tools (no login): loan calculator, market viewer, listings.
  • Account area (login): dashboard, loans/credit management, trading.
  • Instant Settlement of Transactions: All transactions are immediately reflected on the account balance of both parties. Interest not applied on negative balances.
  • Guarantee: Shadowgate Bank stands as counterparty for exchange trades.

Technology

Shadowgate runs on a small, efficient stack designed to be reliable and easy to audit:

  • ShadowLink API — a thin API layer that aggregates and settles trades and transactions.
  • Permanent Data Storage — transaction logs, orders, and settlements are stored in structured sheets users can export at any time.

Design principles

  • Price–time priority in matching.
  • Append-only logs for orders, trades, and ledger events.
  • Deterministic processing (single serialized write path).
  • Transparency via public, view-only market pages.

Roadmap

  • Auth-gated account dashboard and statements.
  • Company listing forms and investor profiles.
  • Candlestick charts and advanced market filters.
  • CSV exports & weekly statements archive.
